Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-06-24 · Effective 2026-06-24

This Privacy Policy describes how the Dopacart iOS app ("Dopacart", "we") handles information. Dopacart is a simulated, for-entertainment shopping experience: you browse a curated catalog and place pretend orders. No real products are sold or shipped, and no real payments are processed.

In short: Dopacart uses no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs and does not track you across other apps or websites. It does show ads through Google AdMob (see "Advertising" below). Otherwise we collect only what's needed to run the app — basic first-party usage data, and (if you sign in) your account, the pretend orders you place, and your saved checkout details so they sync across your devices.

You can use Dopacart with or without an account

You can browse and place pretend orders as a guest, or sign in to save your activity across devices. The information we handle depends on which you choose, as described below.

What we collect

  • Account information (only if you sign in). Dopacart lets you sign in with Sign in with Google or Sign in with Apple. When you do, we receive from that provider a stable user identifier, your email address, and your name. With Apple you may choose Hide My Email, in which case we receive a private relay address rather than your real one. We use this solely to create and recognize your account.
  • Member profile (optional). If you sign in, you can pick a public @username and generate a send code; we store these with your account so other members can send you a (pretend) order. You can change your username at any time in the app.
  • Your simulated activity. The pretend orders and items you place are stored on our server so your activity is preserved across sessions and devices (when signed in) or for the app to function (as a guest).
  • Basic usage data (first-party). To operate and improve the app, our own server records simple events: app opens and approximate time spent, products you view, searches you run, items you add to your cart, and the pretend orders you place and what's in them. We use this only to run app features — your order history and "money not spent" stats, and merchandising such as "most viewed", "most purchased", and "most added to cart". When you're signed in this is associated with your account; otherwise it's associated with a random per-install identifier (see below).
  • Per-install identifier. On first launch the app generates a random "client id" that is not derived from your device hardware and is not your name or email. It lets guest activity and pretend orders work without an account. Deleting and reinstalling the app generates a new one.
  • Push token (only if you enable notifications). If you allow notifications, we store the push token Apple issues for your device so we can send order updates and occasional announcements. You can turn notifications off at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Requests & feedback. If you submit an "add this item" request or send feedback from the app, we store the text you provide (with your account or client id) so we can read and act on it.

Shipping & payment details at checkout

Because the checkout is meant to feel real, it asks for a name, shipping address, and card details. There are no real charges in Dopacart, and the full card number and security code (CVV) you type are never stored and never sent to our server — they exist only in the checkout form on your device.

To make checkout convenient across your devices, if you save a card we keep only its brand, last four digits, expiry, and the cardholder name — never the full card number or CVV. When you are signed in, your saved shipping address and this card metadata are stored with your account on our server so a new device restores them on login; as a guest they stay on your device only. You can remove a saved card or address in the app at any time, and deleting your account removes them from our server.

Sending orders to members

Signed-in members can send a (pretend) order to another member at checkout, identified by that member's email address or send code. We use that email or code only to look up the recipient and deliver the order into their Orders, attributed to your name or @username. To protect members' privacy we never disclose whether a given email belongs to a member — if there's no matching member, nothing happens and the sender isn't told either way. We don't use these emails for any other purpose.

What we do not collect

  • Your Google or Apple password — sign-in is handled by the provider; we never see it.
  • Your location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • Any cross-app or cross-site tracking. Dopacart does not request App Tracking Transparency permission, so ads are non-personalized by default and your advertising identifier (IDFA) is not used to track you across other apps or websites.
  • No third-party analytics, crash-reporting, or marketing SDKs. The only third-party SDK is Google AdMob, used to display ads (see "Advertising").
  • Real payment or financial information — there are no real purchases, and the full card number and security code you type at checkout are never stored or transmitted.

Advertising

Dopacart shows ads through Google AdMob (the Google Mobile Ads SDK). To deliver ads, prevent fraud, and cap how often an ad is shown, Google may collect device information and in-app activity and may use a device identifier. Dopacart does not request App Tracking Transparency permission, so by default ads are non-personalized and your advertising identifier (IDFA) is not used to track you across other apps or websites. Google's handling of advertising data is governed by How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services and Google's Privacy Policy. You can limit ad personalization in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

Leaderboard

If you sign in, Dopacart includes a leaderboard that ranks shoppers by the (pretend) money they "didn't spend" and by their daily streak. You are not shown on it by default — you appear only if you turn on "Show me on the leaderboard" in your profile. When you do, your entry shows your first name and last initial (for example, "Alex T.") — never your email — alongside those numbers, and you can turn it off again at any time.

Favorites sharing

You can optionally share your favorites/wishlist so other shoppers can see and "gift" them. This is off by default; when you turn it on, your shared favorites and your first name and last initial become visible to other users, and you can turn it off again at any time.

Reviews

If you sign in, you can post product reviews. A review is shown publicly alongside your first name and last initial (for example, "Alex T."), never your email. Reviews are moderated: a review you submit is visible only to you until we approve it. Please don't include personal or sensitive information in a review. We may edit or remove reviews and may suspend an account that abuses the feature; you can also ask us to remove a review you posted by emailing us.

App integrity (App Attest)

Dopacart uses Apple's App Attest to confirm that requests come from a genuine, unmodified copy of the app, which protects the service from abuse. App Attest produces a device-level integrity signal only; it does not identify you and is not used for tracking.

Where your data is stored

Account records, pretend orders, reviews, usage events, and — when you are signed in — your saved shipping address and card metadata (brand, last four, expiry, cardholder name) are stored on our own server at api.taskgizmo.com so they sync across your devices. Your cart and app preferences (and, as a guest, your saved address and card metadata) are stored locally on your device and are removed when you delete the app.

Third parties

When you choose to sign in, Google or Apple acts as an identity provider; their handling of the sign-in is governed by their own privacy policies. If you enable notifications, delivery is handled by Apple's Push Notification service. Dopacart shows ads through Google AdMob (see "Advertising"). Dopacart integrates no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and we do not sell or rent your personal information.

Children

Dopacart is a general-audience app. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices & account deletion

You can use the app as a guest, sign out at any time, and turn notifications, leaderboard visibility, and favorites sharing on or off. If you have an account, you can delete it and all associated data from within the app (Profile → Delete account), or by emailing us at the address below. Deleting the app removes the data stored locally on your device.
