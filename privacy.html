Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-06-16 · Effective 2026-06-16

This Privacy Policy describes how the Dopacart iOS app ("Dopacart", "we") handles information. Dopacart is a simulated, for-entertainment shopping experience: you browse a curated catalog and place pretend orders. No real products are sold or shipped, and no real payments are processed.

In short: Dopacart contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only information we collect is what's needed to give you an account and remember the pretend orders you place — and only if you choose to sign in.

What we collect

  • Account information (only if you sign in). Dopacart lets you sign in with Sign in with Google or Sign in with Apple. When you do, we receive from that provider a stable user identifier, your email address, and your name. With Apple you may choose Hide My Email, in which case we receive a private relay address rather than your real one. We use this solely to create and recognize your account.
  • Your simulated activity. The pretend orders and items you place are stored on our server, associated with your account, so your activity is preserved across sessions and devices.

What we do not collect

  • Your Google or Apple password — sign-in is handled by the provider; we never see it.
  • Your location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • Advertising identifiers, device fingerprints, or any tracking identifiers.
  • Usage, browsing, or interaction analytics. There is no analytics SDK, crash-reporting SDK, advertising SDK, or marketing SDK of any kind.
  • Real payment or financial information — there are no real purchases in Dopacart.

Leaderboard

If you sign in, Dopacart includes a leaderboard that ranks shoppers by the (pretend) money they "didn't spend" and by their daily streak. To other signed-in users, your entry shows your first name and last initial (for example, "Alex T.") — never your email — alongside those numbers. If you'd prefer not to appear, you can sign out (the rest of the app still works) or contact us to be excluded.

App integrity (App Attest)

Dopacart uses Apple's App Attest to confirm that requests come from a genuine, unmodified copy of the app, which protects the service from abuse. App Attest produces a device-level integrity signal only; it does not identify you and is not used for tracking.

Where your data is stored

Account records and your simulated orders are stored on our own server at api.pythonenthusiast.com. Your cart and app preferences are also stored locally on your device and are removed when you delete the app.

Third parties

When you choose to sign in, Google or Apple acts as an identity provider; their handling of the sign-in is governed by their own privacy policies. Dopacart integrates no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and we do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers.

Children

Dopacart is a general-audience app. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices & account deletion

You can sign out at any time. You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Settings → Delete Account), or by emailing us at the address below. Deletion removes your account record and the simulated orders stored under it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.

Contact
